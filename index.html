FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before you ship. If your question isn't here, ask directly — we respond fast.

Ask on Discord Telegram
How quickly do I get paid after sending my device? +

Once your device arrives at our facility, inspection takes ~1 hour — same business day for most. Payout goes out via Zelle/Cash App/Venmo/PayPal/ACH within the same day.

Do you accept carrier-locked iPhones? +

Yes. We buy locked AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, Boost, Cricket, etc. — usually 25–30% below unlocked rates. Exact numbers are on the full pricing page.

Is escrow or a middleman available? +

Absolutely. For orders of value, we'll fund the escrow or use a mutually agreed middleman. Just ask on Discord or Telegram to set it up.

What condition does my device need to be in? +

We buy across the spectrum — new, used, even broken for parts. Be honest in your description and we'll honor the quote.

How does shipping work? Do I have to pay for it? +

We email a free prepaid UPS or FedEx label for items over $100. Just print, box up, and drop off. Insurance is included.

What payment methods do you accept? +

Zelle, Cash App, Venmo, PayPal G&S, ACH bank transfer, USDC/BTC. Your pick at quote time.

Can I sell in bulk? +

Yes — wholesale lots welcome. Send a spreadsheet or list and we'll quote within hours. Higher per-unit rates for resellers.
