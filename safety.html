Protections

How every sale is protected.

Four checks every sale runs through. Together they make sure the device is legitimate, your payout is locked, and you walk away with a paper record of the deal.

For your protection

Verified transactions

A quick verification step on both sides locks in who's selling and who's paying. It's how we guarantee your payout actually lands, prevents impersonation or chargeback fraud, and keeps the deal protected end-to-end.

  • ✓ One-time check at point of sale
  • ✓ Both parties protected, never one-sided
  • ✓ Stored securely, never shared, never sold
Clean device guaranteed

Device verification

We run every IMEI and serial through the CTIA stolen-phone registry and confirm Activation Lock, Find My, and Google FRP are cleared before payout. It keeps the resale market honest and means every device we hand out has a clean, traceable history.

  • ✓ CTIA Stolen Phone Checker lookup
  • ✓ Activation Lock / Google FRP confirmed clear
  • ✓ Carrier balance & financing checked
Paper record

Signed bill of sale

At the point of sale you get a signed bill of sale documenting the device, the price, the date, and both parties. That's your proof the transaction happened, that you got paid, and that ownership transferred over to us.

  • ✓ Device make, model, IMEI/serial
  • ✓ Agreed price + payout method
  • ✓ Signed copy emailed to you on the spot

Mission

Keep tech in use.
Out of landfills.

Every device that lives a second life is one less in a landfill and one more in someone's hands. We refurbish what we buy, sell it on at a fair price, and divert real e-waste from the trash. Buying from us, selling to us, both routes keep tech moving, and that's the whole point.

Refurbish & resell

Working devices get inspected, cleaned, data-wiped, and put back on the market at a price that beats new. Verified clean every time.

Divert e-waste

What we can't refurbish goes to certified e-waste recyclers, not the trash. Batteries, boards, rare metals: pulled out and reused properly.

Affordable, verified devices

Every device we resell carries the same verification stack, clean IMEI, locks cleared, tested working. People who can't afford new still get gear they can trust.
