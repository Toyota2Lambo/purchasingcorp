Our Mission

Keep tech in use.
Out of landfills.

Every device that lives a second life is one less in a landfill and one more in someone's hands. We refurbish what we buy, sell it on at a fair price, and divert real e-waste from the trash. Buying from us, selling to us, both routes keep tech moving, and that's the whole point.

The loop

Every device earns a second life.

Whatever you send us goes one of two ways: back into someone's hands, or broken down responsibly for the parts that still matter. Nothing useful sits dead in a drawer, and nothing recyclable ends up in the trash.

Refurbish & resell

Working devices get inspected, cleaned, data-wiped, and put back on the market at a price that beats new. Verified clean every time.

Divert e-waste

What we can't refurbish goes to certified e-waste recyclers, not the trash. Batteries, boards, rare metals: pulled out and reused properly.

Affordable, verified devices

Every device we resell carries the same verification stack, clean IMEI, locks cleared, tested working. People who can't afford new still get gear they can trust.

Why it matters

The greenest device is the one already made.

Most of a phone's lifetime footprint is spent before it ever turns on, mined, shipped, and manufactured. Keeping a working device in circulation skips all of that. A fair payout to you is also the lowest-impact thing that drawer phone will ever do.

  • Reuse beats recycling

    A device resold whole keeps far more value, and far more material, in play than one shredded for scrap. We try resale first, always.

  • Nothing goes to the curb

    When a device truly can't be saved, it goes to certified recyclers who recover the batteries, boards, and metals properly.

  • Access, not just disposal

    Refurbished gear at a fair price means more people get reliable tech they can actually afford. The loop helps both ends.
